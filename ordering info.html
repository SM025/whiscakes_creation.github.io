What are your hours?

All items must be pre-ordered. We are in the shop Tuesday-Sunday with by-appointment hours. Please be sure to call ahead before stopping by.

How much time do you need to make a cake?

Pre-Designed Cakes at least 4 days notice. Custom cakes require at least 1 week notice, some designs require more time. Please Contact us for more information. (link of contacts)

Can I send you a photo of a cake that I'd like you to copy?

Yes, absolutely! Email your photo to us, stop into the shop or upload the picture in this website.

How much do the cakes cost?

Custom cakes start at P 450.00, for a round 4" (serves 4) with a simple custom design. Tiered Cakes will start at $85.00 to serve 25 people. Decorations and custom designs will be subject to additional cost, and are priced by the amount of time that the decor takes for our talented staff to make. Upcharges for design are based on a cake by cake basis.

Upcharges? What are those?

Custom cake prices are based on how much time it takes for us to make the decorations on the cake. Buying a cake is like buying a car: the more upgrades you add, the higher the cost.

How can I order a cakes, or dessert table?

We take orders either by phone, email, or through this website.

Can I customize the dessert table a little more?

You can customize some of our dessert table such as Cupcakes and Cake Pops.

How far in advance should I place my order?

We’d like as much lead-time as possible…but if you have a short notice order, I'm sorry, we do not entertain short notice order. We ask that you give us at least 1 to 5 weeks notice on specialty cake orders. So plan ahead!

Do you have a retail store where I can walk in and purchase a cake on the spot?

No, we are by-appointment only shop which specializes in custom cakes and desserts. All our orders are made for clients and we do not carry cakes on hand.

Can I pick up my order at the store? Where are you located?

Yes! We own a by-appointment only specialty cake shop in Santolan, Pasig City. You may contact us to schedule a time to pick up your cake or desserts. Click here for location information and directions. (link of contact section)
