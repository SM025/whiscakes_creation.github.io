Your cakes are beautiful, but do they taste good too?

At Whiscakes Creation, we make all of our cakes, buttercream, fondant and decorations from scratch. That means we’re baking them when you order them. We only use fresh ingredients, including real butter, milk, flour, sugar and eggs! The fondant we use is a little tough and soft and has the consistency of a marshmallow. We always assure that our cake is not too shaky to eat or too sweet.

This means our cakes not only look beautiful, but they also taste delicious

Are the toppers on your cake edible?

The toppers are made out of fondant. The topper portion is edible but firmer. It is usually have the consistency of a gumball, firm on the outside and soft and chewy in the middle.
